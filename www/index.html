There once was a girl named Avila. She loved to eat fruit, especially berries.

Every morning Avila would have berries for breakfast and spend time looking out the window. This morning was special, Avila had a visitor. A bee named Bonnie.
Avila wondered "what do bees do all day?".

Few weeks later, Avila looked through her window, surprised, a beautiful berry tree had blossomed. Avila thought to herself "How did that plain old tree grow all those berries?"

The next time she saw Bonnie the bee, she asked her the same question and Bonnie answered: "My sisters and I pollinated that tree for you, we know you love berries!".

Days later, Avila was looking through her window and saw her friend Bonnie woozy and about to fall. She asked her what was wrong. Bonnie told her what happened...

"An evil plane sprayed a nasty liquid all over the fields, it got us dizzy and me and my sisters are now lost. We don't know how to get back to the hive",

Avila rushed out to the garden and helped Bonnie and her sisters get back to the hive. After that, Avila convinced her parents to help her set up a bee yard where she would protect Bonnie and her family.

Since that day Avila became a beekeeper, she takes care of the bees and the bees take care of her.
